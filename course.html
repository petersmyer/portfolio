🤔 The Great Debate

Which do you add first? Choose wisely—your breakfast reputation depends on it.

🎉 Excellent Choice

Cereal first = perfect ratio control, less splash, more crunch, calmer mornings.

😅 Bold Choice

Milk first can lead to splashy chaos and ratio mysteries—but we respect trailblazers. Let’s proceed!

🎯 Objectives

  • ✅Follow the proper prep sequence
  • ✅Hit the ideal cereal-to-milk ratio
  • ✅Keep cereal crunchy longer
  • ✅Add optional toppings like a pro

🪜 Steps at a Glance

1
Gather tools — bowl, spoon, cereal, milk.
2
Add cereal — fill the bowl about 2/3 full.
3
Add milk — pour slowly around the edges.
Ideal: just enough to cover the cereal.
4
Optional toppings — 🍌 bananas, 🍓 berries, 🥜 nuts, 🍯 honey.
5
Enjoy immediately — maximize crunch, minimize sog.

🤔 The Great Debate

Which first?

  • 🌾Cereal-first → best ratio control, less splash.
  • 🥛Milk-first → rebel energy… but tougher to judge.

💡 Pro Tips

  • ❄️Colder milk = longer crunch
  • 🌀Pour around the bowl’s edge
  • ➕Start with less milk
  • ⏱️Eat soon after pouring

📏 Quick Ratios

  • 🥣 Bowl 2/3 full of cereal
  • 🥛 Milk to just cover cereal surface
  • 🔁 Add milk in increments as you eat
